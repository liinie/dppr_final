Instructions

The doomsday has come, the earth is going to come to an end. The majority of human beings is evacuated to outer space. You are the only one left on the earth.

You are in panic and searching for something which can take you to leave.

All of a sudden, you find a spaceship. It seems to be still in good shape and function. You also find an instruction manual inside the machine. You have no choice but to learn how to control the spaceship.
