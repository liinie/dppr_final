Instructions

You are going to play a game called Spaceship Adventure. You goal is to control the "spaceship" on the grid world to reach its destination. You can move your "spaceship" stepwise with a certain keystroke. At each step, there is a small chance that your game ends immediately. You will be playing the game with multiple rounds. After each round you reach the goal, the game board is reset.

For each step you move, you will get -1 point, whereas you will get 20 points for reaching the goal. You should try to collect as many points as possible.

This game also contains sound, we suggest you to turn on the speaker or take a headphone.
